# Product Vision

## Purpose

SRCHR is a professional discovery and trust platform for companies, HR teams, agencies, independent experts, and specialist teams.

The product helps buyers find qualified performers, compare expertise, review public proof of work, request estimates, publish tasks, and build a reliable shortlist.

For suppliers, SRCHR helps experts and contractors present expertise, publish cases and articles, collect reputation, receive relevant opportunities, and build a public professional presence.

## Core Idea

SRCHR should not be a simple directory. It should combine:

- public profiles;
- proof of work;
- expert content;
- tenders and price requests;
- events;
- favorites and collections;
- reputation;
- analytics;
- recommendations.

The long-term product direction is a trust layer for professional HR and related services.

## Product Principles

- Experts and organizations are separate entities.
- Expert profile does not replace company profile.
- Organizations can be contractors, clients, or both by capability, not by user account type.
- A user can act personally as an expert or through organizations where they have rights.
- Public content should be discoverable by guests.
- Private contacts, actions, drafts, moderation, and personal data require authentication.
- Moderation protects catalog quality.
- Reputation should be cumulative, explainable, and resistant to manipulation.

## MVP Direction

The first stable product foundation should prioritize:

- clean auth and onboarding;
- contractor and expert public profiles;
- media/materials as proof of expertise;
- tenders and price requests as demand generation;
- favorites and collections as buyer workflow;
- admin moderation;
- notifications;
- analytics and reputation base;
- documentation that prevents architectural drift.

## Non-Goals for Now

- Payment automation;
- complex subscription enforcement;
- chat;
- marketplace escrow;
- ticket sales;
- complex AI matching without enough behavioral data;
- heavy CRM workflows.

## Success Criteria

SRCHR works when:

- buyers can quickly find and compare relevant performers;
- experts and contractors can prove credibility without manual sales decks;
- admins can moderate quality;
- users can save and revisit candidates;
- platform data can support future recommendations and reputation.
