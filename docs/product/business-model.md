# Business Model

## Current Stage

SRCHR is in MVP/foundation stage. The product should collect supply, demand, behavioral data, and trust signals before introducing heavy monetization.

## Value for Buyers

Buyers get:

- searchable contractor and expert catalogs;
- public proof of expertise;
- saved shortlists;
- tasks and price requests;
- contact access after login;
- future recommendations based on context and behavior.

## Value for Suppliers

Experts and contractors get:

- public profiles;
- cases and articles;
- event visibility;
- opportunity discovery;
- reputation accumulation;
- analytics;
- future lead and promotion mechanics.

## Monetization Directions

Potential monetization:

- promoted profiles;
- promoted events;
- promoted materials;
- subscription tiers for contractors;
- advanced analytics;
- lead access;
- tender/price request response limits;
- verified status;
- recruiting or sourcing tools for companies.

## MVP Monetization Rule

Do not block core profile creation, basic expert profile, or basic public visibility too early.

The product needs enough content, profiles, and behavioral data before strict paywalls.

## Operational Model

Moderation is part of the business model. Catalog quality is a differentiator, so not everything should become public automatically.

Admin-managed promotion can exist before payment automation.
